01 Subscription Billing

Authority Lab operates on a recurring subscription model. Payments are processed automatically on your billing cycle (monthly or annually) using your chosen payment method. By subscribing, you authorize these recurring charges.

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you will retain full access until that date.

02 General Non-Refundable Policy

All payments made to Authority Lab are generally non-refundable. This includes payments for the current billing period, any unused portion of a subscription, and any previous billing periods.

We encourage you to use the Free plan to evaluate the Service before upgrading to a paid plan.

03 Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that exceptional situations arise. Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis in circumstances such as:

Significant technical failures caused solely by our platform that prevented reasonable use of the Service for an extended period — and which our support team was unable to resolve in a timely manner.

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the charge in question. We do not guarantee approval of any refund request and reserve the right to make final determinations at our discretion.

04 Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your payment provider without first contacting us, we reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account pending resolution. We ask that you contact our support team first — we are committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly.
