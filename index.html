The methodology

The RCFTO Framework

R
Role
The precise perspective your writing comes from — not just "expert" but the specific angle only you hold.
C
Context
The reader's exact situation — their beliefs, frustrations, what they need to feel understood before they'll trust you.
F
Format
The architecture of your argument — the deliberate sequence that moves your reader toward a conclusion.
T
Tone
Your specific voice — rhythm, register, relationship with the reader. Not "professional" but unmistakably you.
O
Output
What the reader should know, feel, believe, and do by the last word — the measure everything is built toward.
