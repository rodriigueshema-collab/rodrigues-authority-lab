RCFTO Framework
Expert Path · RCFTO Setup

Build your book foundation

Answer these 5 questions. Each one maps to the RCFTO framework that powers the entire book.

R
Role — Your specific perspective
Not just what you do — your exact angle of vision. What have you seen, built, or solved that nobody else in your space has?
C
Context — Your reader's exact situation
Not a category. One person, one moment. What are they struggling with right now, and what do they believe (wrongly) about why?
F
Format — Book structure
How many chapters should carry this argument?
T
Tone — Your voice fingerprint
How do you naturally communicate? Pick the closest match.
O
Output — What should the reader decide?
By the last page, what should your reader know, feel, and do? This is the standard everything else is built toward.
